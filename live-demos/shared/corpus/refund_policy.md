# Northwind Cloud — Refund Policy (v3)

Enterprise customers may request a refund within **45 days** of the order date.
Standard (non-enterprise) customers have a **30-day** refund window.

Refunds **above $200 require manager approval** before they are issued.

Subscription fees already consumed in the current cycle are non-refundable.

To determine eligibility, verify the order's date, status, and customer type in the order system.
