# Coverage Policy CP-115 — Outpatient Physical Therapy (Extended Course)

**SYNTHETIC teaching material. Not a real medical policy. Not medical advice.**

## Scope
Prior authorization for an **extended course** of outpatient physical therapy (PT)
beyond the plan's standard benefit of **12 visits** per condition per year.

## Medical-necessity criteria
Additional visits are **approved** when ALL of the following are documented:

1. **Standard benefit used.** The member has used most or all of the initial
   12-visit benefit for the condition.
2. **Documented functional progress.** Objective, measured improvement toward
   functional goals (e.g., range-of-motion gains, pain-scale reduction, or a
   standardized functional-outcome score) is documented for the completed visits.
3. **A defined, time-limited plan.** The requesting therapist provides an updated
   plan of care with specific goals and a defined number of additional visits
   (typically **≤ 12**).

## Deny when
- No functional progress is documented across the initial course (indicating PT is
  not benefiting the member), and no change in the plan of care is proposed.

## Pend for more information when
- Functional-progress measures are not documented.
- The updated plan of care or the number of additional visits requested is missing.

## Notes
Maintenance therapy (no expected further functional gain) is not covered under this
policy.
