# Enterprise Refund Policy (v3)

Enterprise customers may request a refund within **45 days** of the order date.
Standard (non-enterprise) customers have a **30-day** refund window.

Refunds **above $200 require manager approval** before they are issued.

Digital goods are non-refundable once they have been downloaded.

To determine eligibility, verify the order's date and status in the order system.
An order is eligible only if it is within the customer's window and not a downloaded digital good.
