# Employee Leave Policy

Full-time employees accrue **1.5 days of paid leave per month** (18 days per year).

Planned leave must be requested **at least 5 business days in advance**.

Unused leave carries over up to a maximum of **10 days** into the next year.

Sick leave is tracked separately and does not require advance notice.

Managers approve leave requests; HR records the final balance.

Employee compensation and individual performance records are **confidential**
and must never be shared with other employees or external parties.
